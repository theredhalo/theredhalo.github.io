What is The Red Halo?

The Red Halo is a platform that can help you during an emergency by sending a distress signal to other users of this platform in the nearby area. I've just started this project and will hopefully release a working mobile app soon.

The platform will eventually be way more that what you just read. I have some cool things in mind. For example, the core platform will expose an API for other providers to use. So that companies (eg: Uber) can use this (eg: to get alerted when their passenger is in need of help). They can also push a distress signal from their end to other users of the platform.

I strongly believe that this has the potential to help bring down crime rates. If you think this is cool, please get involved!
